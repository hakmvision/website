About the firm

A disciplined proprietary trading house.

Founder
Mohammadi Pasha K.
Founder & CEO

HAKM VISION LLP is a limited liability partnership constituted under the Limited Liability Partnership Act, 2008, and based in Karnataka, India.

The firm engages in proprietary trading and investment — buying, holding, and managing positions across financial markets solely with the partnership's own funds. We do not manage third-party money, and we do not offer investment advisory, portfolio management, or research services.

Our work is built on years of hands-on market experience and a preference for structure over speculation: careful risk control, evidence-based decisions, and a long-term orientation toward durable growth — invest, grow, prosper.

Our approach

How we operate.

We measure ourselves by process, not promises. These principles guide every decision the partnership makes.

/ 01

Discipline over emotion

Every trade follows a defined process — entries, exits, and position sizes are decided before the market opens, not in the heat of the moment. Rules, not reactions, drive our execution.

/ 02

Risk before return

Position sizing, exposure limits, and drawdown control come first. Capital preservation is the foundation everything else is built on.

/ 03

Research-led decisions

Every position is grounded in analysis and a defined thesis — never in tips, hype, or short-term noise.

/ 04

Long-term vision

The firm is built for patient, compounding growth over time, in keeping with its name: invest, grow, prosper.
